COVID-19 - acute respiratory infection caused by SARS-CoV-2 infection. It was first recognized and described in November 2019 in Central China (Wuhan City, Hubei Province) during a series of cases that initiated the pandemic of this disease. The WHO position based on data from March 3, 2020 speaks of a mortality rate of 3.4%. On January 30, 2020, WHO announced an international public health emergency as a result of the spreading COVID-19 epidemic. On March 11, 2020 WHO recognized the COVID-19 series from December 2019 as a pandemic.
